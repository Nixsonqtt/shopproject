About us

ABOUT US

Flipkart Online Mall is a multi – vendor ecommerce platform in Ghana designed for the small and medium scale businesses. The Online Mall is tailored for the average shop owner in Ghana to own a free online store that can automate their business processes. Retail shops in Ghana can now own a free online shop in less than one minute. Flipkart makes it possible for online store owners to organize products online, customize your store, track and respond to orders, collect payments and have goods delivered at the doorstep of clients.

Our range of services are designed to ensure optimum levels of convenience and customer satisfaction with the retail process; these services include our lowest price guarantee, 7-day free return policy*, order delivery-tracking, dedicated customer service support and many other premium service

As we continue to expand the mall, our scope of offerings will increase in variety, simplicity and convenience; join us and enjoy the increasing benefits. It’s easy to find the right products at a competitive price from our unexhaustive categories for every moment – on your phone, your computer, your tablet and more.

Vision

To become Ghana’s most powerful e-commerce platform that creates live changing experience for buyers and sellers.

Mission

Making live easier for consumers through a lower price click experience.

The essence of Flipkart’s identity and purpose of existence is guided by Simplicity, innovation, Quality, honesty and integrity as our core values.
